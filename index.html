What is Kayletter?

Someone has you made you this custom website to leave you a daily note!

Under your note there is a timer indicating when you can unlock and view your next note.

The date in the bottom left of the note is the date when the note was created.

Highlighted text on a note indicates that it is favorited.


Actions

Single click on arrow: Show next/prev note

Double click on arrow: Show first/last read note

Double click on note: Add note to favorites

Double click on image: Cycle through favorited notes
